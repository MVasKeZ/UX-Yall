Nov 9 • Raleigh Convention Center, NC

UX Y’all is a user experience conference with Southern hospitality. We will celebrate the North Carolina Triangle area, with its diverse and growing design and technical scene. Lots of local UX practitioners speak at conferences and Meetups all over the world, and UX Y’all aims to bring those speakers together right here in downtown Raleigh.

A full day of learning – November 9 – a two-track day of local UX speakers sharing their knowledge and experience.

Join us at the Raleigh Convention Center. More details soon!

We hope to see y'all there!
